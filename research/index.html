Distance as an Access Barrier for Library Usage

I test whether physical borrowing falls as distance to the nearest library increases. The analysis uses municipality-level data and compares alternative distance and population definitions.

Do Higher Gasoline Prices Reduce Road Traffic in Copenhagen?

I examine whether higher gasoline prices are linked to lower road traffic in Copenhagen, using fixed traffic counters, rainfall controls, and robustness checks with lagged and smoothed fuel prices.
