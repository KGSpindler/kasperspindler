Fuel Prices and Road Traffic in Copenhagen

A short empirical analysis of whether gasoline-price fluctuations are associated with lower road traffic in Copenhagen, using fixed counting stations, matched rainfall data, and robustness checks with lagged and smoothed fuel prices.
